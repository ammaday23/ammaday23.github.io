Thulusdhoo Island

Thulusdhoo is located about 28 km northeast of Malé. It has around 1,400 residents and is known for its surfing opportunities. The island is a popular destination for surfers due to its world-class surf break called "Cokes."

Population: 1,400

Famous for: Surfing
