Thulusdhoo Island

Location and Population:

Thulusdhoo is located about 28 km northeast of Malé. The island has around 1,400 residents.

Tourism and Guesthouses: Known as a surfing hotspot, Thulusdhoo is popular among surf enthusiasts, particularly for its world-class surf break known as "Cokes." The island has several guesthouses and accommodations.

Economy: Tourism is the main source of income, especially surf tourism, followed by fishing and traditional industries.

Infrastructure: The island has schools, health facilities, and regular transport to and from Malé via speedboats and ferries.

Environment: Beautiful beaches and vibrant coral reefs make this island a key attraction for tourists.
